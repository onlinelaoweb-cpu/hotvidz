This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how HotVidz uses them, and your choices regarding cookies. Please read this policy in conjunction with our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies cannot harm your device and do not contain personally identifiable information by themselves.

Google Analytics, advertising networks, and affiliate tracking platforms. We recommend reviewing their respective policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

View cookies stored on your device, delete individual or all cookies, block cookies from specific websites, or block all cookies from being set.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site. For instructions on managing cookies in your browser, visit your browser's help documentation.
